Project 03

Cutting Procurement Lead Time With Process Analysis & Simulation

Gap Analysis Business Process Modeling Simulation Modeling Statistical Analysis KPI Analysis Excel

Overview

Schneider Electric's process for sourcing end-of-life components was slow and bottlenecked, dragging out procurement lead time and tying up cost. Over a 7-month engagement, our team mapped the process end-to-end and modeled a faster one.

  • Ran a gap analysis of the existing workflow and KPIs to pinpoint where procurement lead time was being lost
  • Used business process modeling and Monte Carlo simulation to test redesigned scenarios before any real-world change
  • Validated process changes projected to cut procurement lead time by 22.91% and save roughly Php 4M annually
  • Delivered the analysis and recommendations to Schneider Electric as the basis for implementation
